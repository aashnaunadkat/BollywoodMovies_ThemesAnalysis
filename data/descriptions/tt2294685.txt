[Wikipedia Plot Synopsis]
Jeena Hai Toh Thok Daal is an Indian crime drama film that follows the journey of four outlaws from Bihar who travel to Mumbai on a mission. The film portrays the story of mercenaries who have grown up in the world of crime and are united in their passion for thrill-seeking. The film's plot revolves around their pursuit of a crime, which leads them to the city of Mumbai. However, the group's plan takes a dramatic turn when one of them has a change of heart. The film explores the themes of conscience, redemption, and the consequences of one's actions.
